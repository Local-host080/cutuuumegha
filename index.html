A Love Letter to My Cutuu

Together for ❤️

Dear Cutuu,

On September 19, 2024, something beautiful happened. I met a girl who, without even trying, became the most special part of my life. A simple conversation on a dating app led to our first late-night call, and I remember how I couldn't stop smiling the entire time. Your voice—so soothing, so full of warmth—was all I wanted to hear.

Then, as days passed, you started calling me "Cutuu." No one had ever called me that before. And in that moment, I realized something—I wasn't just talking to any girl. I was talking to someone who was going to change my life.

Through endless conversations, laughter, silly fights, and shared reels, you became the person I can't go a day without. Despite the distance between us, you make me feel close, like you're right here with me.

You are the girl with a calm mind and a pure heart. You care for everyone, forgive easily, and understand even when you don’t have to. Your kindness, your warmth, your patience—it’s something rare, something precious.

And then, there’s the way you look—especially when you put a bindi on your forehead and wear traditional clothes. In those moments, you don’t just look beautiful; you look like an angel. The kind of vision that stays in my heart forever.

Day by day, I find myself thinking about you more. You're on my mind 24/7. No matter what I do, my thoughts always find their way back to you. And honestly? I wouldn’t have it any other way.

I know I'm not perfect—I'm just an ordinary guy with flaws. But somehow, with you, I feel complete. You make me better in ways I never imagined. With you, I don’t need to be perfect, because you are the missing piece that makes me whole.

So today, on Propose Day, I want to ask you something that’s been on my heart for so long—

Will you be mine forever, Cutuu? ❤️

YES, I AM YOURS! No, but I still love you!
